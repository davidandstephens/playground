David Stephens is a web developer from San Antonio, TX. Devourer of macaroni and cheese, lover of professional wrestling, basketball fan, and known video game archivist; he never takes himself too seriously. Born in Houston, but a product of Central Texas through and through: David has no problem with being called a gentle man, but might debate you on a scholar.

David is a Junior Instructor at Codeup, a career accelerator program based out of downtown San Antonio. Having graduated from the Xanadu cohort of Codeup, David's skills, and team first attitude could be seen from day one. From helping to debug fellow student's code, to giving his classmates a pick me up with some Werther's, David had always been known to pick others up when they're down. Having built a number of projects, David's scientific approach to development has yielded fun and functional results.
